Why This Investment, Why Now

Aligned with Strada's Core Mission

Nectir intervenes at the precise moment many learners lose momentum ‚Äî day-to-day academic and institutional navigation. By expanding access to 24/7, course-specific guidance, it directly advances Strada's goal of clearing the path between education and work, especially for community college and online learners facing structural barriers.

Quality Coaching at Unprecedented Scale

Strada's Quality Coaching team is enthusiastic about embedding Strada's education-to-career coaching principles directly into Nectir's AI prompts ‚Äî ensuring responses reflect best practices around equity, student agency, and informed decision-making across millions of students.

System-Level Validation with California CCC

The California Community Colleges partnership (116 campuses, ~2.1M students) demonstrates Nectir's ability to execute complex, governed, system-wide deployments. This provides a replicable model for Texas, North Carolina, Indiana, and other state system opportunities.

Governance Moat Compounds as Regulation Grows

As AI use in education becomes increasingly regulated, Nectir's governance-first product design ‚Äî role-based permissions, FERPA compliance, private LLM endpoints, and institutional controls ‚Äî becomes a stronger competitive moat. No direct competitor matches this at system scale.

Capital Efficiency & Prepaid Revenue

Large system-level prepaid contracts turn operating cash flow positive before EBITDA breakeven ‚Äî a rare structural advantage. With $30M+ projected cash reserves by 2028, Nectir eliminates near-term financing risk and gives Strada downside protection.

No Portfolio Conflict

No current Strada portfolio company competes with Nectir's AI infrastructure positioning. This adds a genuinely differentiated capability to the portfolio while creating opportunities for cross-portfolio synergies in coaching, advising, and career navigation.
